# YT 8

1. Remain in Light (Their masterpiece, it needs repeated playing, so much going on)
2. Speaking in Tongues (Dated in sound and production, but besides "Swamp" its fantastic)
3. Talking Heads '77 (Brilliant debut, and worth checking is the CBS (?) 1975 Demos for simpler arrangements)
4. More Songs About Buildings & Food (Some truly intresing Eno overdubs, but 77 Part II Really (?!)
5. Fear Of Music (an clustrophic album done in 4 days, very good though, but not essensial except opener!)
6. Naked (Highly underrated, ditches their 83-87 style and goes back to the 79-83 with a 88 production, great album!)
7. Little creatures (A couple truly fantastic ideas, but come on, its no Speaking In Tongues)
8. True stories (+O.S.T.) (Wild Wild Life is probably the only trly great song for me)
